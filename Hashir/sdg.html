Sustainable Development Goals (SDG)

✨ Introduction

Poverty is one of the most critical global challenges, affecting millions of people who struggle for basic needs such as food, housing, education, and healthcare.
The project Awareness on No Poverty focuses on explaining the causes, effects, and solutions related to poverty, as highlighted under Sustainable Development Goal 1 (SDG 1).
By creating awareness among students and communities, the project encourages collective responsibility to support vulnerable groups, promote equality, and work toward a society where everyone has access to essential resources and opportunities.

✨ Objectives of the Project

To spread awareness about the concept and impact of poverty.
To explain the key goals and importance of SDG 1: No Poverty.
To highlight government schemes and support systems available for poor and vulnerable communities.
To encourage students and citizens to participate in anti-poverty initiatives.
To promote equality, social development, and opportunities for all.

✨ Expected Outcomes

Increased understanding of the causes and consequences of poverty.
Better awareness of government policies and welfare programs aimed at poverty reduction.
Encouragement of community participation in helping underprivileged groups.
Enhanced sense of responsibility and empathy toward people in need.
Improved knowledge about global efforts to end poverty under SDG 1.

✨Conclusion

The project Awareness on No Poverty helps students and communities understand the seriousness of poverty and the importance of working together to reduce it. By learning about SDG 1 and the various welfare schemes available, individuals become more informed and motivated to support and uplift those who are struggling. This awareness is an important step toward building a society where everyone can live with dignity, security, and equal opportunities for a better future.
